Timing of the Events

Fashion week happens twice a year in the major fashion capitals of the world;
New York (February 7-15),London (February 15-19), Milan (February 19-25), and Paris (February 25-March 5) .
In February and March, designers showcased their autumn and winter collections.
In September and October,They showcased their spring and summer collections.
